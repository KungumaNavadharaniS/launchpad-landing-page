16 weeks that change everything

Team formation, problem discovery workshops, and your first mentor match.

Weeks 3–6: Build

Rapid MVP sprints with daily standups and weekly product critiques.

Weeks 7–10: Validate

100 customer conversations. Real revenue or real data. No shortcuts.

Weeks 11–14: Scale

Growth playbooks, hiring your first team member, and fundraising prep.

Weeks 15–16: Demo Day

Present to 100+ investors. The most important 3 minutes of your career so far.
