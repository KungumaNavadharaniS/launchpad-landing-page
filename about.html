Our story

From a dorm room to a global program

Launchpad started in 2019 when Nadia Osei, a Computer Science student at UCL, couldn't find a startup program that took student founders seriously. Every accelerator wanted traction. Every VC wanted a revenue history. Nobody cared about the best ideas — they cared about risk.

She built the first cohort herself. Eight teams. A borrowed meeting room. Five mentor calls over Zoom. Six months later, two of those companies had raised pre-seed rounds, and one was cash-flow positive.

Word spread. By Cohort 3, we had applicants from 40 countries. By Cohort 5, our first unicorn founder (yes, really) had graduated the program. Today, 340+ founders have gone through Launchpad, raising a combined $42M and building companies across every sector.

We're still run by founders who remember what it felt like to have an idea nobody believed in. That's the whole point.

What we believe

Our values

01

Build before you're ready

Waiting for the perfect idea is how you end up with none. Ship, learn, iterate.

02

Talk to customers obsessively

No amount of whiteboard thinking beats one honest conversation with your target user.

03

Radical transparency

We tell founders the hard truths. Good mentors do that. Bad ones tell you what you want to hear.

04

Equity-free by design

We take 0% equity. Your company is yours. We succeed when you succeed.

05

Diversity is a performance variable

The best founding teams look different, think differently, and come from everywhere.

06

Long-term thinking

We care about whether you're still building in 5 years, not just whether you raised a round.
